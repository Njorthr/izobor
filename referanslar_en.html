MOSB ELECTRICITY GENERATION INC.

The steam and heating needs of some of the factories operating in the Manisa Organized Industrial Zone are met by transporting the waste heat obtained from the Natural Gas Combined Power Plant installed in the OIZ with pre-insulated welded steel pipes and fittings.

PEKDEMIR FARM

The heating need of the 120,000 m2 closed area greenhouse of Pekdemir Farm, which is the big chain of supermarkets in Denizli, was met by using geothermal pipes and fittings with a length of approximately 5200 m and a diameter of DN400 for the geothermal fluid in the wells located at a distance of 2600 m.

RİXOS SUNGATE HOTEL

As part of the renovation works of Rixos Sungate Hotel, pre-insulated PPRC pipes and fittings were used in the underground domestic hot water and circulation lines.

CAM SAKURA CITY HOSPITAL

Geothermal pipe for the transportation of heating and cooling fluids of the Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation Hospital, which was built separately from the main hospital block in the Başakşehir Çam and Sakura City Hospital Project, which has a bed capacity of 2,682 and a closed construction area of 1,000,000 m2, made by Rönesans Holding with the Public Private Partnership model of the Ministry of Health. and attachments are used.

TURKISH AVIATION SPACE INDUSTRY (TUSAŞ) - TAI

Existing buildings and new offices, workshops, etc. to be built in the central campus of TAI in Ankara. The heat requirement of the facilities was met by transporting the fluid in the centrally installed hot water system using pre-insulated steel pipes and fittings.

MAREN ENERGY

Operating in Aydın - İzmir regions, one of the Kipaş Holding Energy Group companies, Maren Maraş Elektrik Üretim Sanayi ve Tic. BALCOVA THERMAL HOTEL

SIMAV GEOTHERMAL REGIONAL HEATING

Approximately 72.000 m of pre-insulated steel/PPRC pipes and fittings in DN50-DN400 diameter range were used between 2011-2020 in the city heating system established by Simav Municipality by utilizing the heating energy of geothermal fluid extracted from underground in Simav district of Kütahya and having a temperature of approximately 98°C.

SANDIKLI ONE-STOP DISTRIBUTION PROJECT

Approximately 12.600m of pre-insulated steel/PPRC pipes and fittings in the DN150-DN400 diameter range were used to heat the existing greenhouses and approximately 1,000,000m2 greenhouses to be established in the future by utilizing the heat energy of the geothermal fluid extracted from the underground in the Sandıklı district of Afyonkarahisar and at a temperature of approximately 80°C. .

ZORLU ENERGY

GAZİ UNIVERSITY
